# Amravati Marathon — Event Information

## About the Event

The Amravati Marathon Association has organized this prestigious Half Marathon annually since 2016. With eight successful events completed, the Amravati Half Marathon is recognized for its meticulous organization and participant-focused approach.

**Total Prize Pool**: ₹4,50,000 across all categories

## Event Features

**Inclusive Categories**: The event welcomes runners of all levels:

- Separate age categories for optimal competition
- Special "Out of Maharashtra" category to celebrate national and international participation
- Children's Dream Run (5 km) to encourage young runners

**Race Philosophy**: Participants are the focal point. The event is designed to be welcoming, well-organized, and memorable.

## Race Director

**Dilip Patil** — Race Director

A passionate marathoner since 2003, Dilip has completed 100+ marathons across India and internationally. He is a six-time finisher of world major marathons and a nine-time COMRADES Marathon veteran. His marathons span global destinations: Munich, Prague, Jungfrau, Dubai, Du-Médoc, Amsterdam, New York, Chicago, Abu Dhabi, Boston, and London.

His vision: to make Amravati Marathon a benchmark for organized, inclusive, and celebrated running events.
